# Taking Care of Myself After Leaving the Hospital

Leaving the hospital is an important step. Knowing your plan before you go home
lowers the chance of problems and of having to return.

Keep an up-to-date list of all your medicines, including the name, dose, and when
to take each one. Bring this list to every appointment. If a new medicine
replaces one you took before, make sure you know which to stop.

Know your follow-up appointments and how to get there. Write down who to call
with questions, including a daytime number for your care team and what to do
after hours.

Understand your warning signs. For your condition, learn which symptoms mean you
should call your care team and which mean you should seek emergency care right
away.

Make sure you can manage at home. Arrange help with meals, transportation, and
picking up prescriptions if you need it. Ask for help before small problems
become emergencies.
